CORPORATE INFO

About Us

Founded in 1998, Leonardo Apparel Group located in Medan, Sumatera Utara. The group specializes in woven and knit apparel, with the product range encompassing men's and women's casual wear, dress wear, active sportswear, athleisure, outerwear, and denim.

Vision

To redefine lifestyle and fashion by setting the gold standard for trust, innovation, and sustainability.

Mission

To passionately deliver creative and comprehensive solutions, fostering meaningful collaborations that elevate experiences and inspire confidence.
